# Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Project Overview
As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have created a one-page visual resource designed to help my peers understand the core components  
of Digital Literacy and why these skills are essential for academic and professional success.

## Design Details
* **Tool Used:** Canva.
* **Topics Covered:**
    1. What Digital Literacy is ?
    2. Useful digital tools for students.
    3. Safe internet practices.

### Content Summary
The infographic serves as a quick-reference guide for first-year students to navigate the digital world responsibly. It highlights  
how mastering digital tools can improve study efficiency while emphasizing the importance of maintaining a secure online presence.
